The Koraha Fantasy Basketball League Constitution



Section 1: Rules


1:1 All members must attend the KFBL draft in person unless given a special exception from the KFBL commissioner.

1:2 Season winner will be awarded a ring and given the KFBL trophy for a year. The Winner's team name, name and season year will be engraved on the KFBL trophy.

1:3 Any person to cause damage to the KFBL trophy will be physically punished.

1:4 Buy-ins must be paid BEFORE the draft.

1:5 Prize money pay-out is 70% of the prize pool to first place, 20% to second and 10% to third. The $100 last place penalty will pay for the championship ring.

1:6 Inactivity will not be taken lightly. All members must commit to the WHOLE season.

1:7 Any attempts to undermine the integrity of the KFBL will be severely punished.

1:8 Number of Teams is slightly flexible season to season.

1:9 The team in last place at the end of the REGULAR season will pay an additional $100 into the prize pool.

1:10 There will be NO “gentleman agreements” between members. All members must play each week to the best of their ability. Any suspicious activity will be investigated by league officials.



Section 2: Scoring


2:1 Scoring is based on SEVEN categories.

Field Goal Percentage (FG%)

Three Pointers Made (3PM)

Rebounds (REB)

Assists (AST)

Steals (STL)

Blocks (BLK)

Points (PTS)

2:2 Scoring Type - Head-to-Head: Most Categories.

2:3 Regular Season ties will be broken by most Points (PTS).



Section 3: Roster


3:1 Roster size is TEN. SIX starters. FOUR bench players. ONE injury slot.

3:2 Roster Positions are as follows.

Point Guard (PG) – 1

Shooting Guard (SG) – 1

Small Forward (SF) – 1

Power Forward (PF) – 1

Center (C) – 1

Util (UTIL) – 1

Bench (BE) – 4

Injured Reserve (IR) – 1

3:3 ESPN’s Undroppable Players List will be used.

3:4 All teams will be spilt up randomly between TWO conferences before the KFBL Draft Lottery. KFBL commissioner will do this using a randomization system for their choosing. 



Section 4: Acquisitions and Waivers


4:1 Lineup Changes – Daily – Lock individually at Scheduled Gametime.

4:2 Player Acquisition System – Waivers

4:3 Season Acquisition Limit – No Limit

4:4 Matchup Acquisition Limit – 7 per matchup (for 7-day matchups)

4:5 Waiver Period – 1 day

4:6 Waiver Order – Move to Last After Claim, Never Reset Order



Section 5: Trades


5:1 Trade Limit – No Limit

5:2 Trade Review Period – 2 days

5:3 Votes Required to Veto Trade – 7

5:4 Once a trade is accepted, the trade is FINAL. Only a veto from the KFBL commissioner or 7 vetoes from members can cancel it.

5:6 Teams that have no mathematical possibility of making the KFBL playoffs are no longer allowed to trade.

5:7 Trade deadline is the final day of the regular season.

5:8 The inbuilt ESPN veto function will be the one used for vetoing trades.



Section 6: Playoffs


6:1 Playoff Teams – 6. Top three from EACH conference. Flexible if league is greater than 10 teams.

6:2 Weeks Per Playoff Matchup – 2

6:3 Playoff Seeding Tiebreaker – Head to Head Record



Section 7: Draft


7:1 Draft Type is Snake. Each pick is allocated 120 seconds per pick. 

7:2 Draft pick trading is NOT allowed.

7:3 The KFBL Draft Lottery will be hosted prior to the draft date by the KFBL commissioner using a manual randomized system. The system is decided by the KFBL commissioner.

7:4 Draft date will be set by the KFBL commissioner. The draft date must be prior to the NBA season starting.
